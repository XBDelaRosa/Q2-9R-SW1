Cream Puffs

Cream puffs are balls of French choux pastry that are filled with whipped cream, custard, pastry cream, or ice cream, usually with a sweet and creamy filling.

Price: Php 105

Palmier

Palmier is a French pastry in the shape of a palm leaf or a butterfly, also known as palm leaves, Coeur de France, French hearts, shoe-soles, or glasses.

Price: Php 75

Tart

A tart is a baked dish with an open top that isn't pastry-covered and a filling layered over a foundation made of pastry. Modern tarts are often fruit-based, occasionally with custard, and have a sweet or savory filling. The dough is typically shortcrust pastry

Price: Php 159
